THE STORY OF EUCLIDEAN
We are a group of long-time friends, who came together not in spite of, but because of their different knowledge and interest.
We paint together. We code together. We compose together.
Now we make video games together.

Viewing video games as an art medium, we try to find ways to express new and under-explored ideas as faithfully as possible.

Art and technology combined can do magical things, and we are novice magicians.
